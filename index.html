Catering

Why would you plan an event without Austin Terrier’s delicious pizza, sandwiches or fries! Plan your next event for your office, team or friends – we cater to all sizes, shapes and appetites! Email us and send us all your group details and requirements. You can order directly off the menu or we will develop a specific menu that fits the size of your group as well as their eating preferences and dietary restrictions. Whatever your needs – we will satisfy your group and leave them happy!

About Austin Terrier

Fun, friendly and dedicated is what makes Austin Terrier the reliable place to eat with your family, friends or co-workers.

We offer a creative menu of salads, gourmet sandwiches and pizzas, a diverse kids menu and wide range of craft beers and wines. Using fresh ingredients we create a wide range of tasty options that can satisfy the most eclectic palette. Whether it is a healthy salad, a gluten-free pizza or sandwich, or a hearty burger – we provide menu options for all customer tastes.

Austin Terrier combines a modern dining environment and a vibrant atmosphere to create a fun and satisfying dining experience, all the while providing the highest level of customer service possible. We look forward to meeting you and having you back!
